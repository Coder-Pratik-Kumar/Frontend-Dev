The Art of Typography

Typography is not just about choosing a font; it's about creating harmony in design. The balance of weight, spacing, and alignment determines readability and impact.

Every designer must understand how font families work together. From serif classics to clean sans-serifs, each has its own personality. A well-styled paragraph can transform simple text into a meaningful experience.

Proper line-height and letter-spacing can make even long passages easy on the eyes. Typography is the invisible art that shapes how readers feel about words.
